Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

At Muniverse, we value your privacy and collect only the minimum information required for the platform to function. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Muniverse only collects the following limited information:

  • Account Information: Your chosen username and password, used solely for login and authentication purposes.
  • Public Content: Any posts, ideas, or materials you choose to make public on Muniverse. These are visible to other users by design.

We do not collect unnecessary personal data such as your location, device information, browsing history, or analytics data.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To create and manage your Muniverse account.
  • To allow you to post, share, and interact with content publicly.
  • To maintain basic platform functionality and user security.

3. Data Storage and Security

Passwords are stored securely using encryption or hashing methods. We do not store them in plain text. Public posts are stored in our database and displayed publicly as part of Muniverse’s collaborative environment.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Public Information

Any content you post publicly (such as ideas, comments, or profile information marked as public) may be visible to other users and search engines. Please avoid posting sensitive personal information in public areas.

5. No Third-Party Tracking

Muniverse does not use cookies, advertising networks, or third-party analytics tools. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with external services.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by contacting us. Public posts may remain visible if they have been shared or reposted by others.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if our data practices change. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
